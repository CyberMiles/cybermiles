Rock Star Software Developer

The CyberMiles project aims to build the next generation blockchain-based Smart Contract platform specifically optimized for e-commerce. It envisions a decentralized, Open Source, secure and fair e-commerce ecosystem, where rules and enforcement actions are enshrined in code and algorithms.

The CyberMiles Foundation has recently raised over $30 million USD from top institutional investors and supporters from 119 countries to build its blockchain network and disrupt the trillion-dollar e-commerce status quo. Through its partner, 5miles, CyberMiles is committed to migrate over 12 million US e-commerce users and over $3 billion USD in annual transactions, to the blockchain, making it one of the biggest blockchains after Bitcoin. Join us in this revolution!

If you are a self-motivated software developer who takes pride in your code, loves learning, enjoys difficult challenges, and wants to change the world through your code, we would like to speak with you! A track record of Open Source contributions (Github and StackOverflow) is a strong plus.

True to the spirit of open source collaboration and decentralization, we believe top talents and like-minded collaborators can come from anywhere in the world. We can provide flexible work arrangements (full time, part time, contract-to-hire, or project-based) with top pay, and most of your work will be open source.
